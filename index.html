Welcome to 3E Garage !!!

At 3E Garage, we collect and repurpose electronic components that were once part of student projects, institutional expos, or one-time use prototypes.

Instead of allowing these valuable parts to end up as electronic waste, we offer them to aspiring innovators at minimal cost — encouraging reuse over replacement.

This not only empowers the next generation of engineers and makers, but also reduces the environmental burden caused by unnecessary disposal of electronics.

By giving new life to old components, 3E Electronics takes a meaningful step toward a sustainable, circular electronics economy — minimizing e-waste, saving resources, and promoting a culture of mindful engineering."
